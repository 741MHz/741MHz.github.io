Why Do We Ask Questions?

What is the best kind of cheese to use to catch a bear? Of course it’s Camembert.

What did a piece of cheese say when it looked into the mirror? Halloumi.

Why these jokes in a programming oriented blog? Why?

To make you ask the questions! Kinds do it all the time. And adults should do it more often. But even if one day we explain every physical interaction and scientific law, and hope, and dream, and regret, with a single elegant equation. You can still ask why. Why? Why that equation? Why doesn’t universe operate with some different equation?

So why do we ask a questions?
